This is a simple todo list website!
To add an item, simply click on the input field, enter some text, and hit enter!
To mark an item as complete, click the green 'V' to the side of the item.
To remove an item from the list, click the red 'X' to the side of the item.
To change views or clear all completed items, click the buttons below the input field/todo list.
